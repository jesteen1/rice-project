    pulungal rice

    Parboiling is common, partly due to its beneficial effects on the cooking and storage qualities of rice. Studies also suggest that it may have health benefits beyond the increase in nutritional value.Parboiled rice is especially rich in niacin, providing 4 milligrams, or 23 percent of the recommended daily intake in 1 cup of cooked rice. You’ll also get 19 percent of the daily intake of vitamin B-6. One cup of cooked parboiled rice supplies 2 to 3 percent of the recommended daily intake of calcium, iron, magnesium and potassium.

    apple

    Rich in nutrients: Apples are a good source of fiber, vitamin C, and antioxidants such as flavonoids and polyphenols. They also contain small amounts of other nutrients such as potassium, vitamin K, and manganese. Promotes digestion: The high fiber content in apples promotes healthy digestion and can help prevent constipation. May lower risk of chronic diseases: Some studies suggest that consuming apples may be associated with a lower risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease, certain cancers, and type 2 diabetes. This may be due to the antioxidants and other beneficial compounds found in apples.

    Pacha arisi

    Pacharisi is a traditional Tamil Nadu rice variation. This silky shine, flavorful aroma, lengthy and lithe size, and the delicate flavour had overjoyed rich and poor alike, in all conditions. It is a boiled short-grain rice variety. It does not easily become smooth or squishy due to its classical odour, rich taste, and consistent performance. As a result, it stays whiter for way too long. Ponni Raw Rice has a rich nutty flavour and a fluffy texture and is an improved source of fibre, potassium, calcium, and vitamin B-6.
